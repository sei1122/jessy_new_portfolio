Bio

Born and raised in South Korea and based in West Coast Canada, Jessy Jin is a Hair Artist, Educator, Portfolio Colourist of L’Oreal Pro Canada, and a Master stylist at Pomp and proper salon, in Vancouver BC.


Infused with love for hair and inspiration by art and nature, Jessy’s work has natural and unique yet sophisticated finishes.


Jessy is best known for her artistic eye and thought-infused process for finding style that is right for your hair texture and life style which helps you to effortlessly wear it everyday.


Her intention is always to bring out the best from what you have, and create hair styles that show who you are with her creativity and technical skill. Jessy enjoys creative styles and lived-in haircuts, nonetheless she is an expert Colourist and exceptional Editorial Stylist.
