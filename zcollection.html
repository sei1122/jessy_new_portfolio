COLLECTION

The Reef

2020 Contessa Finalist Collection, Multicultural Hairstylist / BC Hairstylist

I was born in the ocean town of Busan, in South Korea. Even though I spent most of my life in the city, I have always had a deep appreciation for the ocean, which is part of the reason why I fell in love with the west coast of Canada. Regarding this collection, I wanted to bring attention to the story of coral bleaching, by creating the hair which showcases this process.


If you want to learn more about it, please check Ocean Wise

Aurora Borealis

2016 Contessa Finalist Collection, Multicultural Hairstylist
